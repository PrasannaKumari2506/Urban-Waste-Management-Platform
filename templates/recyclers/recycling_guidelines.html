General Handling Guidelines

Safety First

  • Always wear gloves when handling waste
  • Use a mask for dusty or chemical waste
  • Never mix hazardous waste with general waste
  • Wash hands thoroughly after every pickup

Sorting at Pickup

  • Separate plastics, paper, metal and glass on-site
  • Keep electronics separate from general recyclables
  • Organic waste must be in sealed bags
  • Confirm waste type matches the request before accepting

Transport & Vehicle

  • Ensure waste is properly secured during transport
  • Do not overload your vehicle beyond its capacity
  • Cover open waste to avoid spillage on roads
  • Clean your vehicle after carrying organic or wet waste

Conduct at Household

  • Be punctual — arrive at the scheduled time
  • Be respectful and professional at all times
  • Only collect what is listed in the pickup request
  • Mark the pickup complete only after waste is collected
